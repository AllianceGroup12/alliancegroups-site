Removals / Makesafes

End-to-end job management for removal and makesafe services — $1M+/yr.

From a single request to a completed certificate, Compliance Shield manages the entire removals and makesafes workflow. Vetted service providers receive jobs instantly and upload photo evidence on completion.

Instant job requests

Landlords or property managers submit a job request from any device. Verified service providers in your area are notified immediately with job details and access instructions.

Real-time tracking

Track every job from request through to completion with live status updates, provider ETAs, and photo evidence uploaded directly by the technician on site.

Automated invoicing

Invoices are generated automatically on job completion and charged to the nominated billing method. Landlords and agencies receive itemised reports for their records.

FAQ

Your top questions about Compliance Shield, answered.

Compliance Shield supports smoke alarm compliance, pool barrier safety, electrical safety, gas safety, asbestos assessments, pest and termite inspections, water efficiency reports, and more. New certificate types are added regularly in response to regulatory changes.

Onboarding is guided and takes less than 30 minutes for most users. You add your properties, select your compliance obligations, and the platform automatically schedules upcoming inspections and sends reminders. Agencies can import existing portfolios in bulk via CSV or API.

Once a job is requested, Compliance Shield automatically matches it to verified service providers in the area and notifies them. Providers accept the job, complete the work, and upload photo evidence and a completion report directly in the platform. Invoicing happens automatically on completion.

Yes. Tenants receive a secure, read-only link to their property's compliance dashboard. They can view current compliance status, upcoming inspection dates, and download certificates. Landlords and property managers control what information is shared with tenants.
